Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tractor Links we collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our
website [Website URL] and our tractor hiring services (collectively, the "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1.0 Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you use our Services:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • Account Information: Information related to your user account, such as username and password.
  • Payment Information: Credit card or other payment details for processing payments.
  • Information: Government-issued identification documents, such as driver's license or passport, for verification purposes.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our Services, including your browsing activities, search history, and interactions with our website.
  • Geolocation Information: Your approximate location based on your IP address or other location data if you enable location services on your device.

2.0 How We Use Your Information
We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To facilitate and manage the tractor hiring process, including processing bookings, verifying user identities, and communicating with you regarding your transactions.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and address any issues or concerns you may have.
  • Improving Services: To analyze usage patterns, conduct research, and improve the functionality and user experience of our website and Services.
  • Marketing and Communication: To send you promotional materials, newsletters, and updates about our Services, with your consent where required by applicable law.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, and to protect our rights, property, and interests.

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments,

  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe that such disclosure
